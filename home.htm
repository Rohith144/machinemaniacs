MACHINE MANIACS

Machine Maniacs is one of the famous company which is providing world's best DC and AC motors. This company is started by a group of college students who have pursed their bachelor's degree in the PSG College of Technology. This was started in the year 2025 on March 10th.

The main manufacturing of this company are the motors which are used in today's electric vehicles. The webpage created for this company is not only for advertising and marketing of the motors but also for providing a vast knowledge about various AC and DC motors, their features and their applications.
